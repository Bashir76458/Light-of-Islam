Day1:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الجَنَّةَ وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ النَّارِ

Translation: O Allah, I ask You for Paradise and seek refuge from the Fire.

Day2:

اللهم إنك عفو تحب العفو فاعف عني

Translation: O Allah, You are forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me.

Day3: Seeking Acceptance of Fasting

اللَّهُمَّ تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا صِيَامَنَا وَقِيَامَنَا

TRANSLATION:Allahumma taqabbal minna siyamana wa qiyamana. O Allah, accept our fasting and our prayers.
Day 4: Seeking Forgiveness

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُسْلِمِينَ أَجْمَعِينَ

Allahummaghfir li wa liwalidayya wa lil-muslimeena ajma'een. O Allah, forgive me, my parents, and all Muslims.
Day 3: Asking for Strength in Worship

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ وَشُكْرِكَ وَحُسْنِ عِبَادَتِكَ

Allahumma a’inni ‘ala dhikrika wa shukrika wa husni ‘ibadatika. O Allah, help me remember You, be grateful to You, and worship You in excellence.
Day 4: Seeking Guidance

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي وَثَبِّتْنِي عَلَى دِينِكَ

Allahummah-dini wa thabbitni ‘ala deenik. O Allah, guide me and keep me steadfast upon Your religion.
--- Day 5: Seeking Protection from Hellfire اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي مِنَ النَّارِ Allahumma ajirni minan-naar. O Allah, save me from the Hellfire. --- Day 6: Seeking Blessings in Time اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لِي فِي وَقْتِي وَأَعْمَالِي Allahumma barik li fi waqti wa a’mali. O Allah, bless my time and my deeds. --- Day 7: Seeking an Increase in Faith اللَّهُمَّ زِدْنِي إِيمَانًا وَيَقِينًا Allahumma zidni imanan wa yaqinan. O Allah, increase me in faith and certainty. --- Day 8: Asking for Piety اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ الْمُتَّقِينَ Allahumma aj’alni minal-muttaqeen. O Allah, make me among the pious. --- Day 9: Seeking Good Character اللَّهُمَّ حَسِّنْ خُلُقِي كَمَا حَسَّنْتَ خَلْقِي Allahumma hassin khuluqi kama hassanta khalqi. O Allah, beautify my character as You have beautified my creation. --- Day 10: Asking for Righteous Friends اللَّهُمَّ ارزُقْنِي صُحْبَةَ الصَّالِحِينَ Allahumma arzuqni suhbat as-salihin. O Allah, grant me the companionship of the righteous. --- Day 11: Seeking Contentment اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي قَنُوعًا بِمَا رَزَقْتَنِي Allahumma aj’alni qanu’an bima razaqtani. O Allah, make me content with what You have provided me. --- Day 12: Asking for Sincerity اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ عَمَلِي خَالِصًا لِوَجْهِكَ Allahumma aj’al ‘amali khalisan liwajhika. O Allah, make my actions sincere for Your sake. --- Day 13: Seeking Knowledge اللَّهُمَّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا نَافِعًا Allahumma zidni ‘ilman nafi’a. O Allah, increase me in beneficial knowledge. --- Day 14: Seeking a Good End اللَّهُمَّ اخْتِمْ لِي بِحُسْنِ الْخَاتِمَةِ Allahumma ikhtim li bi husnil khatimah. O Allah, grant me a good ending. --- Day 15: Asking for Mercy اللَّهُمَّ ارْحَمْنِي بِرَحْمَتِكَ الْوَاسِعَةِ Allahumma arhamni birahmatika al-wasi’a. O Allah, have mercy on me with Your vast mercy. --- Day 16: Seeking Patience اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ الصَّابِرِينَ Allahumma aj’alni minas-sabireen. O Allah, make me among the patient. --- Day 17: Asking for Rizq (Sustenance) اللَّهُمَّ ارْزُقْنِي رِزْقًا حَلَالًا طَيِّبًا Allahumma arzuqni rizqan halalan tayyiban. O Allah, provide me with lawful and pure sustenance. --- Day 18: Seeking Light in the Heart اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي نُورًا Allahumma aj’al fi qalbi nooran. O Allah, place light in my heart. --- Day 19: Seeking Barakah (Blessing) in Family اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ فِي أَهْلِي وَذُرِّيَّتِي Allahumma barik fi ahli wa dhurriyyati. O Allah, bless my family and offspring. --- Day 20: Asking for Jannah (Paradise) اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنْ أَهْلِ الْجَنَّةِ Allahumma aj’alni min ahlil-jannah. O Allah, make me among the people of Paradise. --- Day 21: Seeking Forgiveness for the Deceased اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِمَوْتَى الْمُسْلِمِينَ Allahummaghfir limawta al-muslimeen. O Allah, forgive the deceased Muslims. --- Day 22: Seeking Love of Allah اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ حُبَّكَ أَحَبَّ إِلَيَّ مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ Allahumma aj’al hubbaka ahabba ilayya min kulli shay’. O Allah, make Your love the most beloved thing to me. --- Day 23: Asking for Success in Both Worlds اللَّهُمَّ آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan. O our Lord, grant us goodness in this world and goodness in the Hereafter. --- Day 24: Seeking Strength in Faith اللَّهُمَّ ثَبِّتْ قَلْبِي عَلَى دِينِكَ Allahumma thabbit qalbi ‘ala deenik. O Allah, keep my heart firm upon Your religion. --- Day 25: Seeking Protection from Evil اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِي شَرَّ مَا خَلَقْتَ Allahumma ikfini sharra ma khalaqt. O Allah, protect me from the evil of what You have created. --- Day 26-30: Special Laylatul Qadr Dua اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي Allahumma innaka ‘afuwwun tuhibbul ‘afwa fa’fu ‘anni. O Allah, You are Most Forgiving, and You love forgiveness, so forgive me. --- These Duas can help you stay spiritually connected during Ramadan
